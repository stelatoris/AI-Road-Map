Statistics and Probability

Descriptive Statistics

Descriptive statistics is a branch of statistics that deals with summarizing and visualizing data. Topics to focus on include:

  • Measures of central tendency, such as mean, median, and mode
  • Measures of dispersion, such as range, variance, and standard deviation
  • Histograms, box plots, and scatter plots

Inferential Statistics

Inferential statistics is a branch of statistics that deals with making predictions and drawing conclusions about a population based on a sample of data. Topics to focus on include:

  • Hypothesis testing and significance
  • Confidence intervals
  • Regression analysis

Probability Theory

Probability theory is a branch of mathematics that deals with the study of random events. Topics to focus on include:

  • Probability distributions, such as normal, binomial, and Poisson
  • Conditional probability
  • Bayesian probability

Statistical Learning

Statistical learning is a branch of machine learning that deals with the development of algorithms that can learn from data and make predictions. Topics to focus on include:

  • Linear regression
  • Logistic regression
  • Decision trees
  • Random forests
  • Support vector machines
  • Neural networks
  • Clustering
